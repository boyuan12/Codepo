![](https://res.cloudinary.com/boyuan12/image/upload/v1600375245/Screen_Shot_2020-09-17_at_1.40.38_PM_qyznvn.png)

In general, to view Pages, the URL is `/p/<username>/<repository_name>/<path>/`.
