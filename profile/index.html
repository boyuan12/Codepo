Profile

You could also manage your public profile that is publicly available to everybody. To see your own Public profile, go to /<username>/

To edit your profile, you can click on Edit Profile. And you will be redirect to an ugly interface, where you could add some of your personal information, change your Profile picture, etc. (P.S. Have I mentioned that I use Grammarly?)
